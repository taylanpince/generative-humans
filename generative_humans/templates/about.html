What's this about?

Generative Humans is a little experiment on collaborative storytelling, by Taylan and Mimi. For over a year now, our professional and personal lives have been forever changed by large language models that are capable of coming up with creative and interesting stories, as well as imagery. Mimi and I thought, could a group of humans come up with just as interesting, and maybe even more creative stories?
